Our Expertise

Comprehensive Pathology Services

We provide a wide range of diagnostic tests using state-of-the-art equipment to deliver precise and timely results.

Blood Tests

Complete blood count (CBC) and specific blood assays for accurate diagnosis of various conditions.

Urine Test

Comprehensive urinalysis for detecting infections, kidney problems, and metabolic disorders.

Sugar Test

Fasting, post-prandial, and HbA1c testing for precise diabetes screening and tracking.

Thyroid Test

T3, T4, and TSH profiles to ensure your thyroid gland is functioning optimally.

Cholesterol Test

Detailed lipid profiling to assess your cardiovascular health and prevent heart risks.

Liver Test

Liver Function Tests (LFT) encompassing enzymes and proteins for vital organ health.

Kidney Function Test (KFT)

Kidney function tests check how well your kidneys filter waste from the blood. These tests include creatinine, urea, and other markers to diagnose kidney disorders.

Vitamin Deficiency Test

Vitamin tests help detect deficiencies such as Vitamin D, Vitamin B12, and other essential nutrients. These tests are important for maintaining bone health, immunity, and energy levels.

Hormone Test

Hormone tests evaluate the levels of different hormones in the body. They help diagnose hormonal imbalances related to fertility, metabolism, and overall health.

Infection & Disease Screening

Screening tests help detect infections and diseases early. These include tests for dengue, malaria, typhoid, hepatitis, and other infectious conditions.

Pregnancy Test

Pregnancy tests detect the hormone hCG in blood or urine to confirm pregnancy accurately and quickly.

Full Body Health Checkup

Our full body checkup packages include multiple diagnostic tests to evaluate your overall health. These packages help detect health issues early and ensure preventive care.
